Cost of the ticket

What DASA actually costs, by category

Fees vary by institute, but the structure is consistent: a one-time registration fee, a per-semester tuition fee that depends on your category, and hostel/mess charges billed separately.

These figures are illustrative, not official. Actual tuition varies by institute (IIIT Hyderabad, for instance, publishes its own separate DASA fee schedule) and changes yearly. Treat every number on this page as a planning estimate — confirm exact figures in the current year's official brochure before budgeting.

Beyond tuition

Living costs you'll pay on top

Hostel and mess fees are billed by the institute, separately from tuition, every semester.

Housing

Hostel charges

Commonly in the range of ₹5,000–8,000 (roughly US $60–100) per month, though this varies significantly by institute and room type.

Food

Mess charges

Typically ₹4,000–6,000 (roughly US $50–75) per month for a standard mess plan, paid semester-wise before the term starts.

One-time

Other costs to plan for

Security deposits, caution money, student ID/library fees, and visa/travel costs are separate from academic fees — build in a buffer for your first semester.
