Itinerary

Your DASA UG journey, checkpoint by checkpoint

The whole process runs online and is entirely rank- and choice-driven — there's no interview. Here's every stop between sitting JEE Main and moving into a hostel room in India.

1
Jan – Apr

Sit for JEE Main

DASA has no separate entrance test. Register for JEE Main through the NTA like any other candidate, sit for one or both sessions, and get your All India Rank (AIR). This rank is the single biggest lever in the entire process — everything downstream runs off it.

2
Late June – July

DASA registration opens

Create your profile on the official DASA counselling portal using your JEE Main application credentials. Fill in your category (FN/OCI/PIO/NRI/CIWG), personal details, and upload the required scanned documents in the specified format and size.

3
During registration

Pay registration fee

A non-refundable registration fee (commonly around US $300, less for SAARC/CIWG categories) is payable online via SWIFT, e-payment, NEFT or RTGS — as a single transaction with one transaction ID.

4
Late July – early August

Fill and lock your choices

Rank every institute-branch combination you'd genuinely accept, in true order of preference — not "safe" order. Unsaved choices are not auto-saved, and at least one saved choice is required to be considered for allotment. If you don't lock choices by the deadline, your last recorded selection is used automatically.

5
August

Seat allotment rounds (typically 2+ rounds)

The system matches your AIR against your locked choices and available seats. After each round you can accept-and-freeze, accept-and-float (stay open for a better option in the next round), or decline. Check the portal regularly — no separate email/SMS is guaranteed for every update.

6
Within each round's window

Pay balance fee & verify documents

Once allotted a seat, pay the remaining first-semester fee and complete document verification (original documents may be required at physical/online verification, depending on that year's process).

7
September

Spot round (if seats remain)

Candidates unallotted after the regular rounds, or who declined an earlier offer, can register for the spot round — including fresh applicants who meet eligibility. A fresh registration fee applies if you're re-registering after declining.

8
Before joining

Apply for your student visa

Foreign nationals use the provisional admission letter from your allotted institute to apply for a student visa at the nearest Indian Mission. Start this early — visa processing timelines vary widely by country.

9
Semester start

Report at your institute

Complete final reporting at your allotted institute, pay hostel and mess charges (billed separately, semester-wise), and begin classes. English is the medium of instruction across all participating institutes.

Paper trail

Documents to have scanned and ready

Get these ready before registration opens — resizing and rescanning under deadline pressure is where most applicants lose time.

Identity

Passport & photo ID

  • Valid passport (bio page)
  • Recent passport-size photograph
  • Scanned signature
Academic

School records

  • Class X mark sheet & certificate
  • Class XII mark sheet & certificate (or provisional, if awaiting result)
  • Birth certificate
Entrance exam

JEE Main proof

  • JEE Main admit card
  • JEE Main rank card / scorecard
Category-specific

Status proof

  • OCI/PIO card (if applicable)
  • Proof of 2-year study abroad (NRI category)
  • Parent's Gulf employment visa/work permit (CIWG)
Formatting

File specifications

  • Match your name exactly across passport, marksheets and JEE Main result
  • Follow the exact size/format specified in that year's brochure
  • Keep documents clearly readable — blurry scans are a common rejection reason
Before you submit

Preview before you pay

Always use "Preview Application" before fee payment and before final submission — corrections get harder once you've paid and locked choices.

Already hold a JoSAA seat? You can still register for DASA as a fresh candidate. If you're allotted a new DASA seat, your JoSAA seat is automatically cancelled — factor that into your choice order.
One more thing

Budget for the full cost, not just tuition
