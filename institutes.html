Where a DASA seat can actually take you

Around 62 institutes participate in DASA UG each cycle: 31 NITs, IIEST Shibpur (the coordinating institute), 18 IIITs, 3 SPAs, and roughly 35 other centrally-funded technical institutes (Other-GFTIs). IITs are never part of DASA.

What each institute type actually means

"NIT" and "IIIT" aren't just names — they signal different governance, fee structures, and sometimes even a slightly different application route.

  • NITs — National Institutes of Technology, the backbone of the DASA quota, uniform DASA process across all 31.
  • IIITs — Indian Institutes of Information Technology; most follow the common DASA process, though a couple (like IIIT Hyderabad) run partly independent admission tracks — always check the specific institute page.
  • SPAs — Schools of Planning & Architecture, relevant if you're applying for B.Arch/B.Plan rather than B.Tech.
  • Other-GFTIs — "Government Funded Technical Institutes" outside the NIT/IIIT system that opt into the DASA seat-sharing pool each year.
